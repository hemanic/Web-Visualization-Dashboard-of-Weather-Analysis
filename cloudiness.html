Latitude vs. Cloudiness



Observations


The process selected 546 unique cities. There seemed to be no particular correlation between latitude and the cloudiness a city experienced, as cloudiness was fairly evenly distributed. It is interesting to note that the two highest clusters regarding cloudiness were around 0% and 100%, followed by approx 78% and 40%.
