Latitude vs. Wind Speed



Observations


There does not seem to a strong correlation between latitude and wind speed, but it is interesting to note that the highest wind speeds are associated at the top of the northern hemisphere (approx 70-80 degrees). Overall, the majority of cities' windspeeds were clustered within 0mph to 15mph.
